Bonus: Styling

Bonus: Styling

  1. In the head section of your website add this tag just above the closing head tag:

    <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://maxcdn.bootstrapcdn.com/bootstrap/3.3.7/css/bootstrap.min.css" integrity="sha384-BVYiiSIFeK1dGmJRAkycuHAHRg32OmUcww7on3RYdg4Va+PmSTsz/K68vbdEjh4u" crossorigin="anonymous">

  2. Save your file, go to Google Chrome and reload.

We utilized a styling framework called Bootstrap to help us do some quick styling today and tied it to our html with the link tag in our head section.

Join us next month if you want to learn more about styling and how it works!
